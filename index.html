ALLAMA IQBAL EDUCATIONAL AND SOCIAL ORGANISATION is an Non Governmental Organization is located in Hyderabad Telangana. AIESO is registered Society at HYDERABAD, Telangana with Ngo unique registration ID 'TS/2009/0009552'. The NGO registration is done by Registrar of Societies with registration number 1260 on the date of 08-09-2006, The Chairman of AIESO is MOHD NASEERUDDIN and Chief et functionary officer is MOHD NASEERUDDIN. Promoters are MOHD NASEERUDDIN, KHATIJA NAZIMA, RAFEEQ AHMED SABRI, SAYEED AHMED SABRI, MOHD SARFARAZ AHMED.Major Activities and Achievements: Providing both Education Religious & Modern education to the Children & Adults, Upliftment of the Society, Free Interest Loan, Public Lectures, compaign Against Alcohol, Conducting Online Quizzes etc.
